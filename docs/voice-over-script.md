# Voice Over Script — LitFlow AI

**Duration:** ~2 minutes
**Tone:** Professional, informative, confident

---

Every researcher knows the struggle: endless tabs, hundreds of abstracts, and the nagging feeling that the real research gap is hiding somewhere you haven't looked. LitFlow AI is an AI-native research copilot that answers one question — "What should be researched next?" Not a search engine. A five-stage pipeline that takes a single topic and produces a complete, citation-ready literature review with zero back-and-forth.

Here's how it works. LitFlow plans your research questions and keywords, then pulls papers directly from Crossref and arXiv — no API keys, no third-party services. It extracts methodologies and findings from every paper, then analyzes the entire corpus to surface concrete research gaps: areas over-studied, under-explored, or outright contradictory. All in one workspace, all automated.

No configuration, no build step, no server. LitFlow AI is a pure static app that runs on your Anna account — just sign in and go. For researchers who want to stop searching and start discovering, it's the only tool that tells you what comes next.
